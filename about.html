What this is

Unmitigated Wisdom began in 2020 as an independent, insight-driven forecasting channel — a private record of falsifiable claims about the parts of the world most liable to break: great-power conflict, energy markets, nuclear thresholds, proxy wars, and the quiet structural trends underneath them. Over time it has grown into a mixture of original insight-based calls and formal expert-aggregation modelling (Bayesian scenario models, ordnance trackers, accuracy backtests).

Every post is a claim someone could be wrong about. Every claim is dated, sourced, and eventually scored. The point isn't cleverness; it's accountability — a public track record kept in the open.

Methodology

Predictions are only counted when they meet four standards:
  • Falsifiable — a concrete outcome that can be checked.
  • Forward-looking — made before the event, not after.
  • Specific — scoped enough to fail.
  • Attributable — tied to a named source with a citation.
